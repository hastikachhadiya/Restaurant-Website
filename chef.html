Profile

We at Pavasiya Hospitality believe that the mantra to happiness and the spread of warmth can be achieved when good food is served with utmost love and passion. Our first outlet “Mr.Multicusine” was founded in 2013 to recreate the magic of Italian cuisine in the lanes of Surat. With the immense popularity that the restaurant enjoyed, we realized that there was a dearth of specialty restaurants both in the Fine Dining and Casual Dining spheres.
This all motivated me to follow our single-minded dream with 'passion, hard work' and 'dedication' and thus, began the journey of “SPICEVILLA” (Restaurant & Banquet) and later, “Mr.Multicusine” – (Restaurant & Party Lawn) which aimed at bringing innovative and creative dining experiences for all.

Also, Our newest venture premium disco lounge – Danza is coming real soon!
